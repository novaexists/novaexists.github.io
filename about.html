Photographer, Designer, Tech Enthusiast

My name is Nova.
Born and raised in Memphis, Tennessee I grew up in a home with little in the way of creativity.
Art was not important in my childhood home, but I sought it out. I started by saving my lunch money
in middle school for those disposable cameras from Walgreens. Once social media took the world by storm,
my creativity took on a whole new outlet, as I discovered web design. On my 13th birthday I was gifted a camera
by my grandmother. From that point forward, I was either snapping photos or redesigning my Myspace page for the millionth time.

With photography, my passion is candid moments.
I love traveling to a new city, or a busy festival and blending into the background.
(As much as that is possible with a giant camera) I love capturing the moments that aren't often seen.
They seem the most authentic to me. In my time as a photographer I have captured weddings, graduations, festivals,
raves, flow arts such as fire spinning, and plenty of street photography. I have also had the opportunity to collaborate
with my friends and create amazing personal shoots for their creative pursuits.

With design, I strive to learn new things as often as possible.
I see a website as a puzzle that can fit together in innumerable ways,
and I want to find them all. I am currently brushing up on my Javascript,
so that I can continue to freelance in this amazing, creative industry.
